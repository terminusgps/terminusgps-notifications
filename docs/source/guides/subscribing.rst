Subscribing
===========

#. :ref:`Login <logging-in>` to the Terminus GPS Notifications platform.

#. Navigate to the `account`_ page.

#. Add a payment method to your account.

#. Add a shipping address to your account.

#. Navigate to the `subscription`_ page.

#. Select your desired payment method and shipping address.

#. Click the subscribe button. Note this will charge the credit card you specified in the previous step.

After subscribing, your `dashboard`_ will be updated to reflect your active subscription.
